Modern neural theorem provers (e.g., DeepSeek-Prover, AlphaProof) rely on MCTS with
separate policy and value networks.
We hypothesize that a single Energy-Based Model (EBM) can replace both:
the energy score serves as the value function (lower = more promising),
and Langevin dynamics provides a principled sampling mechanism for tactic generation.
