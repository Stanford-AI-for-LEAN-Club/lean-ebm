\begin{abstract}
TODO: write abstract.
We propose using Energy-Based Models (EBMs) trained with Langevin dynamics as a unified
policy and value function for MCTS-based Lean~4 theorem provers.
Lower energy corresponds to more promising proof tactics, replacing the separate
policy/value heads used in systems like DeepSeek-Prover with a single learned energy function.
We prototype this idea on MNIST and benchmark on miniF2F.
\end{abstract}
